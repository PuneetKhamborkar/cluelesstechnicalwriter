# Yet Another Technical Writing Blog?
Do we really need another boring TW blog talking about AI and technical writing, how AI is going replace TWs, how AI is not going to replace TWs, blah blah blah?

## Yes or No

As the name of this website suggests a no-brainer approach. I am pretty clueless what is going to happen next.

## My Take

Just go with the flow.

Try to navigate through the waves, keep swimming, keep hustling. The aim should be to get all the foundation and basics of the technical writing correct.

Once that is done. Try to upskill as much as one can. Learn coding, devops, AI, and what not.
