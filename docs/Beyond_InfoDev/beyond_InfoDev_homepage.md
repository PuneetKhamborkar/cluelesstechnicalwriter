# Beyond InfoDev: Elevate Your Career from Technical Writing to Product Leadership

You’ve mastered technical writing. Now it’s time to level up your career to business analysis, product ownership, or digital leadership. Beyond InfoDev is your guide to professional growth with practical skills, job-ready frameworks, and industry examples.

## Why this page matters

- Targeted guidance for technical writers seeking senior roles
- Real-world career transition strategies for Analyst and Product Owner paths
- Skill mapping: documentation, stakeholder communication, analytics, and product strategy

## What you'll discover

- Career vibes: Technical Writer vs. Business Analyst vs. Product Owner
- Core skill upgrades: requirements elicitation, user research, and team leadership
- Resume & LinkedIn templates for career pivots
- Interview preparation: case studies, work samples, and data-driven messaging

## Start your transition

1. Audit your current writing portfolio and document process improvements.
2. Map your skills to business goals (OKRs, customer insights, product metrics).
3. Build a transition plan: training, mentorship, and incremental role expansion.
4. Use InfoDev resources to fill knowledge gaps in Agile, UX, and analytics.
